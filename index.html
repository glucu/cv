Programming

My journey to programming started in late 2017 when I read a book called, the self-taught Programmer the definitive guide to programming professionally by Cory Althoff. I later came to realize that programming is the most interesting thing in the world. Think of a board game like chess, but much more involed, where you can make up your own rules by constructing a computer to do what you tell it do without it complaining! The thrill of problem solving led me across C++ in 2018 and fell in love with its low-level abilities and it being a statically typed language.
